About Me

Hello, thank you for stopping by! My journey started back in middle school when I met my friend: Maxwell J. Torres. Maxwell kicked off my love for technology and eventually introduced me to the world of computer programming. Maxwell and I spent countless nights talking about one day being computer programmers together. After high school, Maxwell continued pursing this path via enrolling in college.

In 2012, Maxwell passed away in a hit-and-run motorcycle accident. I spent years feeling completely lost. Years later, I finally decided to pursue my ultimate goal of being a web developer while also paying respect to my fallen friend. Here's to you, Maxwell, I hope I'm making you proud. I love you, brother.

Over the years, I have gained experience in a plethora of industries, including retail, retail management, construction, HR work, and manufacturing. I've also co-owned a brick and mortar store with one of my closest friends. When I'm not creating an app or learning something new, you can catch me walking around reptile shows, writing poetry, enjoying stand-up comedy shows, and going on adventures with my girlfriend.

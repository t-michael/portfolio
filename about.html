About Me

Hello, thank you for stopping by! My journey started back in middle school when I met my first friend: Maxwell J. Torres. Maxwell kicked off my love for technology and eventually introduced me to the world of computer programming. Maxwell and I spent countless nights talking about one day being computer programmers together. After high school, Maxwell continued pursing this path via enrolling in college. I did not.

Unfortunately, in 2012, Maxwell passed away in a hit-and-run motorcycle accident. As you can imagine, I didn't handle this well. I spent years feeling completely lost and empty. Years later, I finally decided to pursue my ultimate goal of being a web developer while also paying respect to my fallen friend, thus I enrolled into a full stack web development bootcamp through the University of Minnesota. Here's to you, Maxwell, I hope I'm making you proud. I love you, brother.

When I'm not creating an app or learning something new, you can catch me riding a motorcycle, walking around reptile shows, writing poetry, enjoying stand-up comedy shows, and going on adventures with my girlfriend.
